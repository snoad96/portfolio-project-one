    What is a Circuit

    Circuit Training is one of my favourite exercises to do to improve my running. Because it doesn't have to involve running!

    It can be defined as a consecutive series of timed exercises performed one after the other with varying amounts of rest between each exercise.
    It can be totally personalised and modified to your needs and capabilities.

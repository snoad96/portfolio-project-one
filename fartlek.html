What is Fartlek Training

Fartlek Training is similar to interval training, with moments of high intensity and small rests

In Swedish fartlek literally means speed play. It can be defined as moments of fast running with periods/breaks of slower jog or walking.

Like the other two, it can be totally personalised and modified to your needs and capabilities.

Exercise 1

  1. Warm up- easy jog for 5-10 mins
  2. Hard/fast run for 2km
  3. Speed walk 5 min
  4. Easy jog with;
  5. Sprints every 50m or every other lamp post
  6. Speed walk every 200m or 4th lamp post
  7. Continue this until tired
  8. Light easy jog 10 min
  9. Brisk walk 5 minutes
  10. Cool down stretches

Exercise 2

  1. find some hills
  2. sprint hard up the hill
  3. light jog downhill
  4. walk to next hill or incline
  5. sprint hard up hill
  6. continue until fatigued
  7. fast walk 10 mins

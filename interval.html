What is Interval Training

Interval Training Involves a bunch of high intensity workouts in between moments of rest

Similar to circuit training. But more intense and more based around running based activities.

This can also be totally personalised and modified to your needs and capabilities.

  5. 20 second comfortable jog
  6. 20 second sprint
  7. 20 second comfortable jog

What is Interval Training

Interval Training Involves a bunch of high intensity workouts in between moments of rest

Similar to circuit training. But more intense and more based around running based activities.

This can also be totally personalised and modified to your needs and capabilities.

Exercise 1

  1. (10 minute jog) warm up
  2. 30 second sprint
  3. 20 second jog
  4. 25 second sprint
  5. 20 second jog
  6. 20 second sprint
  7. 20 second jog
  8. 40 second sprint
  9. 20 second jog or walk
  10. (10 minutes jog) cooldown

Exercise 2

  1. Slow jog (10 minutes)
  2. 1 minute 5k pace
  3. 30 seconds slow jog
  4. Repeat 4 times
  5. 2 minute 10k pace
  6. 1 minute slow jog
  7. Repeat 3 times
  8. 3 minutes half marathon pace
  9. 90 seconds slow jog
  10. Repeat 2 times
